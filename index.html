About

Decentraland is an open-source initiative to build a decentralized virtual reality. Blockchain technology is used to claim and transfer land, keeping a permanent record of ownership.

Alpha Prototype: Stone Age

Stone Age is the first proof of concept of the Decentraland Project. Land is modelled as a simple grid, and a Bitcoin-like proof of work algorithm is used to allocate pixels to users. It's a browser-only client featuring a visual representation of the virtual world. Try it now.

Roadmap

Bronze Age

Move from a grid to a 2.5-dimensional world. Add textures, items, scripting to the land you own.

Iron Age

A full-fledged 3D world, with full Oculus Rift support and customization of the laws of physics. Live in the blockchain.
