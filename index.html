Decentraland

A blockchain-based virtual reality world

Decentraland is an open-source initiative to build a virtual reality world. It uses blockchain technology to claim and transfer virtual land, keeping a permanent record of ownership.

Explore Decentraland

Why Decentraland?

A public VR world should be ruled by open standards, shielded from the agenda of any central organization.

In Decentraland, users are land owners, and have full control over the content of their tiles.

To make this possible, Decentraland uses blockchain technology as a permanent immutable record of ownership.

Who is this for?

Artists

To create immersive experiences for anyone in the world to enjoy.

Developers

To push the limits of what is possible in virtual reality interactions.

Online Communities

To gather in an open virtual world and share experiences.

FAQs

Why is Decentraland important for VR?

For VR to reach its full potential, users need to own the content they build. Blockchain technology is a tool that enables a decentralized digital record of ownership, so it seemed like the perfect tool to create a fair VR world were no-one is in control.

Does it run on top of Bitcoin's blockchain?

No. Decentraland utilizes its own blockchain and network, and thus requires some users to run mining nodes.

How is land assigned to miners?

A miner chooses a random tile to mine. It needs to be adjacent to existing land.

How can I become a land owner?

You can become a land owner either by having someone transfer you some of their tiles, or by mining a new one.

How can I create content for my land?

You can use Unity3D Editor to create and publish your tile to the Blockchain. Check out these instructions or this video to see how to do it.

Does it support Oculus Rift or HTC Vive?

The current version doesn't support it, but we are almost there! You can still explore Decentraland here.

Is Decentraland open source?

Yes, it is. We ❤️  open source.

Who validates transactions?

Miners validates transactions. The network rewards miners by assigning them land. Decentraland uses proof of work, just like Bitcoin.

How often is land created?

Land is created on every new block. Time between blocks is 10 minutes for testnet. For livenet it will be ~6 hours.

How is world content distributed?

In the Decentraland blockchain, transactions can transfer the ownership of a given tile, as well as announce the hash of the scene content of the tile. The actual content of a tile is distributed in a P2P manner, via the torrent network.

What kind of content can I add to my land?

There are some limitations on what you can do on your tile. For the moment, the browser implementation only supports primitive meshes (Cube, Plane, Sphere, Cylinder and Capsule), plain colors and textures.
