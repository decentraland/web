About

Decentraland is an open-source initiative to build a decentralized virtual reality. Blockchain technology is used to claim and transfer land, keeping a permanent record of ownership.

Beta Prototype: Bronze Age

Bronze Age is the second proof of concept of the Decentraland Project. Land is modeled in a 3-dimensional space, a Bitcoin-like blockchain is used to allocate land to users and allow them to change it's content. Try it now.

Roadmap

Iron Age

Add multiplayer support with avatars and live chat. Allow custom items and scripting on the land you own.

Silicon Age

A full-fledged 3D world, with full VR support and customization of the laws of physics. Live in the blockchain.
